Weather Dashboard

The dark UI uses a deep navy palette to reduce eye strain, letting the animated weather icons pop. Inter was chosen for its exceptional legibility at small sizes, since weather data needs to be readable at a glance.

The card layout groups related stats (humidity, wind, feels-like) into a single visual unit, reducing the cognitive load when scanning multiple values at once. The cyan accent color signals interactivity and references the water and sky theme of the app. Skeleton loaders hold the card layout in place during API calls, preventing layout shift and making the app feel fast even before data arrives. The unit toggle was placed in the header rather than near each reading to keep the data area clean and uncluttered.

Shine The Way Daycare

A warm cream and sky-blue palette was chosen to signal safety, trust, and care. The hero uses a two-column layout to keep the primary booking CTA visible above the fold without pushing the photography off screen.

The rounded font style reinforces the approachable, child-friendly tone without feeling unprofessional. The primary "Book a Visit" button uses a solid blue fill to clearly stand apart from the outlined "Contact Us" secondary action. Reducing the contrast on secondary CTAs lowers the commitment pressure for undecided visitors. Real photography of children learning was used over illustrations because authentic imagery builds faster emotional trust with parents. A minimal top navigation keeps the layout clean and guides attention toward the single most important conversion action.

JR's Home Cleaning

Bold stacked headlines convey three core brand values in under two seconds. A blue-to-transparent gradient over the hero photograph keeps text legible while preserving the lifestyle imagery behind it.

The green WhatsApp button was intentionally styled to match WhatsApp's own brand color because South African home service businesses rely on WhatsApp as the primary booking channel, so it needed to feel instantly familiar to local users. Inter in a heavy weight was chosen for the hero headline to project confidence and reliability, two qualities a customer needs to feel before inviting a service team into their home. The Cape Town Table Mountain visible in the background anchors the brand geographically, building immediate credibility with local visitors. A sticky "Book Now" button in the navigation ensures the conversion path is always one click away regardless of scroll position.
